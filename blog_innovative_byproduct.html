Innovative By-Product

I trained 1,500+ employees on storyboarding business-presentations. Most of them comprised fresh recruits who learnt the skill as part of their induction training program.

Problem arose when multiple students, then billed employees, requested me to review their actual client presentations. After reviews I realized that though basics of storyboarding were few, multiple employees had forgotten them. I decided to build communities to solve this rampant problem.

I helped and re-trained the first few employees who approached me. I encouraged them to work with any new requestor from their account before approaching me. This community grew and worked like a charm. New trainers were effective with industry-specific feedbacks. Quality of presentations improved across the organization. My workload declined.

Remember, when you make something, you make something else too. I primarily trained recruits but also enabled the creation of communities. Stay observant to spot any opportunity to create a by-product.
