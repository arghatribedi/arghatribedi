Effective Knowledge Transfer

Anyone who has ever received knowledge transfer from me can attest that I never do so without a whiteboard. Writing while explaining has three key benefits:

Makes the teacher think clearly: Mind is programmed to take the least effort route and skip through potentially important aspects. Illustrating ideas forces brain to detail.

Reduces learner's note taking efforts: Learner can pay undivided attention to the session if s/he is not distracted by note-taking. Fun fact: mobile camera has educational purposes too!

Removes abstraction and forces concurrence: Imagine you describing a bear and listener visualizing a chilled beer. Bit too extreme but you get the point. Both parties should be in agreement of messages.
