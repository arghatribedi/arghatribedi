Phone a friend NOW

I remember when Sushant Singh Rajput died, a lot of folks posted on social platforms saying they are available for a call anytime anyone needs them. The problem is mental health patients find it difficult to open up about their issues with anyone. Also, a lot of people carry on smiling while suffering from depression. It’s difficult to spot such a person in day to day life.

Hence do not wait for someone to reach out to you for help. Call a friend today you have not spoken to in months, send a thank you text to someone who helped you a long ago or leave a kind comment on a friend’s post you liked. It will brighten up their mood and help them push through another day. You never know whose life you might save!
