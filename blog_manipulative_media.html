How media manipulates you

Tussle between American media and politicians has been a highlight for the last few years. Both these entities frame messages to influence an individual’s perception of reality ( I am not talking about fake news).

Post 9/11, a message called ‘terror as crime’ was changed to ‘war on terror’. The word war warrants military involvement in foreign territory. Military ‘escalation’ in Iraq was framed as ‘surge’ since the former is permanent and latter is temporary. ‘Exploration of energy’ was used instead of ‘drilling for oil’. Never criticise the ‘government’ which cleans your streets and fights crime. Instead, attack ‘Washington’ for its greed and corruption.

Do not let selective choice of words make you think differently.
