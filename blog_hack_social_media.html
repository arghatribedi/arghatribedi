Hack your social media

Which is higher? Number of crows in India or number of articles calling social media the devil. I am not using your screen space to blame social media. It has its de-merits but you can hack it (in a non-malicious way) for your benefit. A year back, when I was severely over-weight, a college friend took me under his wings. He got me hooked to healthy eating and running.

Running was difficult to start and harder to continue. At this point, social media worked like fuel for me. I dabbled with a few running videos to correct my technique. More I watched, more of my feed was occupied by similar content. As they say, we become what we consume. After realizing the effect I instantly started following a lot of runners and running club pages on Instagram. Constantly seeing people running on every social media platform kept pushing me to run more.

My point - if you want to learn art, unfollow all so called ‘influencers’ and only follow artists. Train the recommendation algorithm to work in your favour.
