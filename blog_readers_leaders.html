Readers are not leaders

Unpopular opinion, readers are not leaders. Writers are. Writing offers multiple proven benefits. I have identified two in my brief experience of blogging:

One, writing makes me think clearly. I can ponder over a problem, it's root cause, and possible solutions at length. But the ideas never solidify until I write them down. I am better equipped at accepting or rejecting my thoughts when I see them. Imagine Sherlock Holmes visualising ideas not in his mind palace but on paper.

Two, writing exposed my poor vocabulary. I was accustomed to apply the word "start" for every beginning ("start" of pandemic, "start" of civilisation). Upon observing repetitive "starts" in my draft, I craved for better words. Hence I discovered the synonyms such as "onset"(for unfavourable events, onset of pandemic) and "dawn"(for favourable events, dawn of civilisation).

Conclusion, read but write more. Leaders are creators, not mere consumers.
